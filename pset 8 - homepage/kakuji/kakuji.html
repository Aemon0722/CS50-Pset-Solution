Kakuji
Traditional Penmanship from Japan

What is Kakuji?

Kakuji is a graphicart that expresses letters (kanji) with almost horizontal and vertical lines only. Although the design looks simple at first glance, yet it encompasses the aesthetic of the Japanese in the Edo era, also known as “Iki”, and playfulness has long been inherited to this day, Kakuji with its simplicity and depth is great to use for designs such as trademark, hanko stamp, family crest and signboard.
